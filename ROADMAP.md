# LegacyShield Roadmap

_Prioritised easiest → hardest. Updated 2026-03-02._

## TIER 1: Quick wins (days, no architecture changes)

- [ ] **Video intro/explainer** — 60-90s screen recording walkthrough. Embed on homepage. Zero code, massive trust signal.
- [ ] **"What if LegacyShield shuts down?" page** — Answer the biggest trust objection. Data export, encryption keys stay with user, open-source commitment. Add to FAQ + dedicated page.
- [ ] **Emotional copy refresh** — Add "What happens if..." storytelling section to homepage. Real fear → real solution. (Inspired by Owner.One's approach)
- [ ] **Domain protection blog post** — "How to Prevent Your Domain from Being Hijacked After Death" — great SEO topic, zero code.

## TIER 2: Medium effort (1-2 weeks each)

- [ ] **Dead Man's Switch** ⭐ THE MOAT — Check-in interval (weekly/monthly), grace period, auto-notify emergency contacts with vault access. This is what separates us from "encrypted Dropbox". Owner.One charges premium for exactly this.
- [ ] **Advisor/Accountant access roles** — New role type: "professional" with read-only access to selected doc categories. Opens B2B: notaries, financial advisors, accountants as users.
- [ ] **Data export tool** — One-click encrypted backup download. Answers continuity concern AND builds trust. "Your data is never hostage."

## TIER 3: Bigger bets (weeks-months)

- [ ] **Domain guardian feature** — Monitor domain expiry dates, alert owner + emergency contacts, instructions for transfer. Unique differentiator nobody else does.
- [ ] **Swiss entity / jurisdiction** — Switzerland = gold standard for privacy trust. Research Swiss foundation or GmbH. Branding: "Swiss-grade privacy, European infrastructure."
- [ ] **Continuity-first entity design** — Legal structure designed to survive the founder. Foundation, trust, or cooperative model. (Nathan interested in brainstorming this)
- [ ] **Multi-user family accounts** — Owner + spouse as co-admins, children as members with tiered visibility. Individual tool → family platform.

## TIER 4: Future vision

- [ ] **International Will integration** — Partner with legal services for cross-border will creation (validated by Owner.One's premium tier)
- [ ] **Self-hosted option** — Docker image, your own server, same UI. For the ultra-paranoid.
